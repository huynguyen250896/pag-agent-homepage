Overview

PAG-Agent is an LLM-powered, web-based virtual research assistant that enables researchers to analyze and interpret transcriptomics data through natural language interactions. PAG-Agent supports transcriptomics data generated by a wide range of sequencing and profiling technologies, including microarray platforms (e.g., Agilent and Affymetrix) and RNA sequencing (RNA-seq), at both bulk and single-cell resolutions. It unifies the entire transcriptomics workflow, including analysis tasks (e.g. data preprocessing, differential expression analysis (DEA), pathway analysis (PA), consensus analysis, meta analysis), as well as post-analysis tasks (e.g., biological interpretation, hypothesis generation, and literature-supported evidence retrieval). Furthermore, PAG-Agent also introduces context-aware Over-Representation Analysis (cORA), a context-aware PA framework that facilitates pathway prioritization and biological interpretation by incorporating biological context.
